About Fantasy World

Fantasy World is an abandoned theme park located in Lemery, Batangas. It was built back in 2001. There were a lot of speculations about this abandoned theme park. Some say it was supposed to be the DisneyLand of the Philippines, while others believe the Japanese businessman who owned it couldn't secure the funds to complete the project.
